About

Compassionate RPN with extensive experience supporting geriatric and vulnerable populations. I integrate clinical practice, health informatics, and strong interpersonal skills to deliver safe, dignified, patient-centered care.

My nursing journey began in my family’s charitable medical center in Egypt, where years of volunteer work taught me that true healing is rooted in empathy, dignity, and human connection.

Through diverse Canadian clinical rotations in acute care, surgery, and complex medical units, I have developed a calm, grounded, and patient-focused approach. Colleagues often describe me as supportive, composed under pressure, and always positive.

I believe in continuous learning—driven by a long-term goal of clinical excellence, leadership, and lifelong growth. I aim to learn something meaningful every day and use that knowledge to enhance patient outcomes.

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.” — Mahatma Gandhi

My purpose in healthcare is simple: to help people feel safe, cared for, and seen—bringing comfort to families during their most vulnerable moments.
